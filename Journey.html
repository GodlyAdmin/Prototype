Welcome to my Gaming Journey

Where/when I Started

I first played Rocket League in September 2022 slightly before I heard about Valorant from a friend. After a month of Rocket League I started playing Valorant. I dropped Rocket league and only played Valorant for a few months. I took a 3 month break in 2024 because of school work and work. Now I am playing both games with a few friends until I have more time to grind.

My Experience

I have around 300 hours in Rocket League and over 900 hours in Valorant. My peak rank in Rocket League is Diamond 2 division 4 (Heatseeker) and in Valorant it is Diamond 1. My main game as of recently is Valorant.
